Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share information online about the white water rafting company. It will allow the target audience to find and learn more about this rafting company. The target audience can then make an informed decision about booking an adventure with the white water rafting company.

Audience

(1) Who are the target customers? The target customers are any able bodied person who enjoys thrilling outdoor activities. They have a little bit of money to spend on fun excursions and enjoy spending time outdoors, appreciating the nature that surrounds them. (2) What do they want? They want to feel the thrills and excitement of adventure, specifically outdoors. They enjoy the beauty of the nature around them and want to be as close to it as possible. (3) How will my audience usually access my site? The audience will typically use a mobile device or a laptop to access my site.
